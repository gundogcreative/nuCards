Our Core Experience

Our core experience comes primarily from direct marketing and direct sales techniques. We understand that, no matter what, sales drive the bottom line. We’ve also come to appreciate that sales without marketing is a lot like piloting a sailboat without sails. During our initial efforts we quickly realized traditional new homeowner marketing efforts produced saturated and sub-par leads. That’s when we decided to start from scratch, break out the drawing boards and create something fresh.
