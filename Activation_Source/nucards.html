nuCards, or what some say are similar to traditional retail gift cards, is our flagship new homeowner marketing program. We’ve seen multiple businesses quadruple in size and gain million dollar revenue increases while utilizing nuCards in the Welcome Box.

What are nuCards?

nuCards are limited redeemable cards similar to retail gift cards which allow select individuals the opportunity to take advantage of awesome offers, utilize quality local businesses and save thousands!

Hint: Look for the lower right hand corner dipped in nuCards blue!

nuCards are selectively delivered to new homeowners after close of escrow, just in time for them to take advantage of all the goodness within each Welcome Box. All nuCards are delivered in our custom Welcome Box. It’s just icing on the cake.

New homeowners can’t get enough nuCards at their door step! We want you to be a part of the Welcome Box! We’re always looking for the best local service providers, recognized national chains and the small mom & pop shops that don’t market past the local neighborhood.

We’ve got solutions for businesses of every shape and size.
