We’re pioneering new homeowner marketing techniques and are excited for you to learn more about our adventure! Below, you will find some basic information about our company and why our marketing program has been such a success.

New Homeowners

Moving into a home is a big commitment. Making sure your new house feels like home is what drives new homeowners to spend more money than existing homeowners in just the first few couple months of living in their newly acquired real estate.

72% of new homeowners will spend $10,000 - $60,000 in just the first year! New homeowners have an immediate need to purchase products & services. We introduce your business to new homeowners at the most crucial point of this process.

How It Works

Custom nuCards

We start with a custom designed nuCard. Your nuCard is hand delivered, along with a selection of other nuCards, to new homeowners in your targeted area within (on average) 1-10 days after close of escrow. We track all deliveries with the most state of the art tracking software so we know exactly when and where each card was delivered.

nuCard Activation

New homeowners can either call in or use our online activation portal to activate the card and utilize the limited offers. After the card(s) have been activated, they’re ready for use and all calls are forwarded on to the respective business or cards are ready to be redeemed in person while visiting a place of establishment.

Smart Call Tracking

We track all inbound calls with a DID (Direct Inward Dialer) over VOIP unique to each business. We know exactly how many calls each card is getting, when they’re coming in and where they’re coming from. This is what we like to call smart marketing data (SMD).

Why It Works

New Homeowners Aren't Shoppers

Would you rather market to a shopper or a buyer? We prefer the latter. New homeowners are buyers, not shoppers. They’ve proved that by purchasing their new home. If they were able to purchase a home, they have a quality credit score and are financially in good standing.

No Purchase Loyalty

Moving into a new home, neighborhood and community is a life changing event. There are so many aspects of everyday life that change when a new home is purchased. From where you get groceries to your new favorite gas station. This is the best time to establish purchase loyalty from new homeowners.

Increased Expenditures

The costs of purchasing a new home do not stop after you unpack the moving truck. At this point, the cost of home ownership is just getting started. Expenditures after closing are an accepted part of the new home process and continues to be part of owning a home year after year.

Direct Mail is Inadequate

The difference between direct mailing “coupons” and hand delivering a Welcome Box of “nuCards” is what puts our new homeowner marketing efforts above the rest. We welcome new homeowners into the neighborhood with our Welcome Box of nuCards that gives the direct mail competitors no chance. We’re like the Welcome Wagon™, but on Steroids.
